The Scarcity of Obesity

Global Adult Obesity Rates 2023

This map shows the percentage of adults with a BMI of 30 or higher in 2023. Unfortunately, not all countries are included in this analysis due to missing data, such as Antarctica. High obesity rates stand out clearly in North America (the United States and Mexico) and parts of the Middle East and North Africa (like Saudi Arabia and Egypt). These regions appear in darker orange-brown shades, indicating obesity rates above 30 to 40%.

Obesity Rate vs Sugar Consumption

Sugar can be an amending tool to help with lowering an individual's obesity. With the limited data available in terms of countries, it's suggested that for the most part, countries that engage in a lower average sugar consumption (kg per capita) have lower obesity rates. However, this is a weak association, and high-obesity countries appear despite relatively modest sugar intake.

Trend of Average BMI in Australia by Sex

This line chart shows how the average BMI in Australia has changed over time for males, females, and the population overall. The steady upward trend across all groups highlights the gradual increase in average BMI, reflecting changes in lifestyle and dietary habits over the years.
